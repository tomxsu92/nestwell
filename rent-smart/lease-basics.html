Lease Basics

Understand what you’re signing before you commit.

What Is a Lease?

A lease is a written agreement between you and your landlord. It explains what you agree to pay, how long you’ll stay, and what both sides are responsible for.

It’s not just paperwork. It’s the foundation of your housing stability. Before signing anything, it’s worth slowing down and understanding what it says.

Why It Matters

Your lease affects your monthly budget, your security deposit, your credit (if payments are missed), and your ability to move out early.

When you understand your lease, you protect your money and your future options. Clarity removes stress.
