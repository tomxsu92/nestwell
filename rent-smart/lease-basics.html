Lease Basics

Understand your lease before you sign.

What Is a Lease?

A lease is a legally binding contract between you and your landlord. It outlines your rights, responsibilities, payment terms, and rules.

Key Things to Look For

• Rent amount and due date • Security deposit terms • Maintenance responsibilities • Early termination clauses • Renewal terms

Why This Matters

Understanding your lease protects your money, your credit, and your housing stability.
