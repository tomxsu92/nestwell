Renters Insurance

A small monthly cost that protects everything you bring into your home.

What Renters Insurance Does

Renters insurance protects your personal belongings if something unexpected happens.

Fires, water damage, theft, or certain natural events can destroy or damage the things you own. Renters insurance helps cover those losses.

What It Usually Covers

  • Furniture
  • Electronics
  • Clothing
  • Personal belongings
  • Temporary housing if your apartment becomes unlivable

Your landlord’s insurance protects the building — not your belongings.

The Good News

Renters insurance is usually very affordable.

Many policies cost around $10–$20 per month depending on your coverage and location.

For many renters, it’s one of the simplest ways to protect their finances.
